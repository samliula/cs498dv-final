The following chart displays U.S. or statesâ€™ trends in confirmed cases. The curve was smoothed out with a rolling 7 day average. To show if the curve is trending up or down, we compared the short term average (rolling 7 day average) with the long term average (rolling 21 day average), so that if the short term average is higher than long term average, it means it's trending up, and vice versa. The red dot means it's trending up, while the green dot means it's trending down.

Hover your mouse over to the dots and see the key stats of your state of that day: daily cases and daily deaths.

You can either view the U.S. trend or an individual state's trend on this chart. To view an individual state, go to one of the other pages and select the state. Or uncheck the state on one of the other pages to view the U.S. trend. Give it a try, and see how different it is in different states.
